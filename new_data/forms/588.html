APO/FPO ADDRESS TIPS

How do I order and ship to an APO or FPO address?

APO/FPO shipping is provided through USPS. They recommend entering the full name with a middle name or initial and PSC number, unit number, or ship name.

You will need to leave the country as United States or you will be charged the foreign shipping rate. Replace the city name with "APO" or "FPO".

Select one of the following for the State in the dropdown list:
APO/FPO AA (Armed Forces of America)
APO/FPO AE (Armed Forces Europe)
APO/FPO AP (Armed Forces Pacific)
