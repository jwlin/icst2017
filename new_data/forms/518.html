Earning Points

You earn points for purchases less credits, returns and adjustments (“Net Purchases”) made by you and/or any authorized user of the Account as follows:

• You earn 5 points for every $1.00 spent on eligible Net Purchases made at priceline.com®. Eligible purchases include: cruises (as defined by the merchant category code), Name Your Own Price®, Express Deals™ and Pay Now bookings, as well as purchases of Trip Protection products, made on priceline.com. A Pay Now booking is any booking that requires payment at the time of booking.  For more information on Name Your Own Price, Express Deals, and Trip Protection, please see priceline.com.

• You earn 1 point for every $1.00 spent on all other Net Purchases.

• Balance transfers, cash advances (including cash equivalent transactions such as, but not limited to, the use of your Account  to obtain money orders, traveler’s checks, foreign currency and lottery tickets), fees, interest charges and unauthorized/fraudulent purchases.

• When a purchase or transaction is not a whole dollar amount, any fraction equal to or greater than $0.50 will be rounded up to the nearest whole dollar and any fraction equal to or less than $0.49 will be rounded down to the nearest whole dollar to calculate your points earned. For example, if you make a purchase in the amount of $25.49, you will be awarded 25 points, but if the purchase or transaction amount is $25.50, you will be awarded 26 points.

Statement Credit Redemptions

•  priceline.com statement credits:

•  priceline.com statement credits are the best value.

• Eligible priceline.com purchases of $25 or more posted to your Account within the last 120 days can be redeemed for statement credit redemptions at a 1.5% value. Eligible purchases include: cruises (as defined by the merchant category code), Name Your Own Price®, Express Deals™ and Pay Now bookings, as well as purchases of Trip Protection products, made on priceline.com. A Pay Now booking is any booking that requires payment at the time of booking.

• Non-priceline.com statement credits:
