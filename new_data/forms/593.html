Easy sunglass shopping means never having to pay for shipping. So at sunglasshut.com, we provide free 2-day shipping on every order.

SHIPPING CONSIDERATIONS

  • All orders are subject to review and could require additional processing time.
  • Weekends and holidays are NOT considered business days.
  • Any orders placed after 2PM EST on Fridays will be processed and ship on the following Monday.
  • For all orders placed after 2PM EST, please allow one additional day for processing. Orders will ship the following day after processing.
  • Carrier delays are possible during high order volume periods and may affect shipping times.
  • Shipments to US Territories may take 1-2 additional business days.
  • Premium Engraving orders may take an additional 3-5 business days for processing.
